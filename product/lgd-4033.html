What is LGD-4033?

LGD-4033 is a potent, selective androgen receptor modulator that has been designed to help individuals [§2] suffering from muscle wasting diseases by elevating muscle mass. This compound may be an appealing alternative to MK-2866 because Ligandrol does not have the same effects on prostate-specific antigen levels, which may rarely lead to cancer risk factors. Researchers have also found this compound to be relatively safe and effective in increasing lean body mass for patients [§2] undergoing treatment, without any concerning side effects such as increased prostate-specific antigens.
